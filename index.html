How to Play

Type a ship’s first letter to lock the closest dangerous match. Complete its word before it reaches Earth.

Acquire a target

Type any letter. The nearest dangerous ship beginning with that letter is selected, and the same keystroke counts as its first letter.

Stay locked

Once locked, only that word accepts letters. Correct letters fire lasers. Incorrect letters lower accuracy and may reduce combo.

Esc Pause

Pause or resume the current run. Escape no longer cancels a word target.

Target controls

Use the on-screen Next Match and Cancel Target buttons when you want to change or clear a lock.

Abilities

Activate Time Freeze, Plasma Pulse, and Shield Repair by clicking their on-screen buttons.

Menu navigation

Use the arrow keys to move focus and press Enter to select the focused option.

Defense line

Incoming damage drains shield first, then Earth health. Shield recharges after several safe seconds.

Campaign

Survive ten waves and defeat the Void Harvester. Choose an upgrade after every second wave.

Endless

Continue through increasingly difficult procedural waves. A stronger boss appears every tenth wave.
